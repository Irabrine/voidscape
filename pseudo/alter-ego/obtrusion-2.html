Elizabeth leaves her white car in the parking lot at the supermarket and goes in to do the shopping for the week, it is Sunday after all.
The moment she’s no longer to be seen, a black van pulls up next to her car, inside are three buff men, the driver is holding binoculars looking at the entrance of the supermarket.
“Alright boys, same as usual, pull her in before she unlocks her car.”
“I feel like we should be careful, this one is unusually expensive, even for the rates of this city.”
“Yeah, I’m with Ray on this one, let’s be as quick as possible.”

Ten minutes pass with the three men patiently waiting for Elizabeth to come back when suddenly they hear a knock on the van door.
One of the two in the back opens the door and sees a little boy in front of them wearing a backpack.
“Excuse me, have you seen my mum, she told me she was going to pick me up but the car is empty.”
“Oh, she just went shopping while waiting for you, come in, stay with us while we wait for your mum, we’re her friends.”
“Oh, okay thanks.”
The kid walks into the van and sits down.

The driver flips over a file he has on his hand while the kid fiddles with his backpack and the other 2 men talk softly to the kid.
“So, how old are ye, wee lad?”
“I’m nine years and four months old.”
“And did you come from a school? I didn’t know one was around here.”
“No, I just had an assignment.”
The driver turns back with a confused look on his face.
“I didn’t know Lizzy had a kid, what’s your name, little one.”
“My name’s Zero, and you didn’t know she had a kid because she doesn’t have one.”
The men’s facial expression changes and before they could react, the kid, with inhuman speed, pulls out a gun from his backpack and shoots three times, a light coming out of the gun hits each of the men’s subclavian veins.
One of them lunges at the kid but falls unconscious nearly immediately on him, and the other two also fail at reacting as they’re all now knocked out.
The kid tapes the driver’s hand to the wheel and straps his neck to the head cushion so he doesn’t fall off; he pulls out a thick metallic disk with a red button in the middle and six claws coming out of it, and he puts it around the wheel.
Four claws hold the disk in place and after the kid pressed the red button, the other two claws dug into the car to access more control.
He pulls out a phone and tinkers with it a little, afterwards, he leaves the van putting his phone away.
The van drives off as the kid skips away into the supermarket.
Half an hour later, Elizabeth leaves the supermarket with a full trailer towards her car, she unpacks everything in the trunk and puts back the trailer to where it’s supposed to be.
She drives away unaware of anything.
